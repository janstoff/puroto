who and what we are...

At Puroto Store, we make innovative technology accessible for everyone to experience. A whole range of products can improve our daily lives by making them easier, more efficient - then again some gadgets are simply just a whole lot of fun.  That being said, these unique products often go unnoticed because creators either lack the public platform or because the product benefit is difficult to put in words. Our aim is removing all these barriers on products we deem special, to enable people to try out and embrace various unique technologies and their benefits for the present and the future.
